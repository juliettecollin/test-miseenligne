Back Trente
2018
Trente is a storage system designed for the furniture brand Conforama. It adapts to the different spaces and needs of its users. The shelves are simple and inexpensive to manufacture. They are made of modest but high quality materials. The system consists of three basic elements : brackets, standards planks and sticks.  Each shelf is designed to be easy to assemble. The system offers two types of brackets. The first one helps the user to assemble the shelves, the second one ensures the stability of the structure. Each part of the system is aligned in a 30 by 30 millimeter grid. The grid allows the user to create an infinite number of combinations. With Juliette Gueganton.
